My Biography

Family Background

I am from a little town called Yala in Cross River State, Nigeria. I was born into a loving, God fearing family. I experienced the loss of my father at a young age. Raised by my mother alongside my siblings,It wasn't always an easy journey, but we never stopped striving and believing in making our lives great. I grew up in a loving home where discipline and strong moral values were paramount. God was also a huge part of our lives. I was thought to always do the right thing and this values have stuck with me.

Education and Early Life

From a young age, I excelled in both academics and sports. I was an active participant in various sports, such as football, long jump, high jump,running, during my primary school years. My academic prowess was equally impressive, as I took part in science competitions and gifted children scholarship exams. I completed my primary education at Mater Dei International Nursery and Primary School in 2005 and continued my secondary education at Nigerian Navy Secondary School, Borikiri, Port Harcourt were I also graduated with distinctions. I furthered my education at the Federal University of technology, Owerri (FUTO) where I studied computer science. I am currently studying Cloud Engineering at AltSchool Africa. It's been an amazing journey so far.

Career Aspirations

I am currently pursuing my studies in Cloud Engineering at AltSchool Africa, With a keen interest in DevOps engineering, I am dedicated to becoming one of the top DevOps engineers in the industry. My commitment to my education and career goals is a testament to the strong emphasis my parents placed on the importance of education. I aspire to put my name out there in the tech space,help to shape the future of DevOps, help solve problems, contribute to innovations and make the tech world a better place in turn making the world a much better place as well.

Interests and Hobbies

Outside of my academic and career pursuits,I enjoy a variety of activities. I am an avid football fan, both playing and watching the sport. My upbringing instilled in me the values of discipline and strong morals, which continue to guide me in my personal and professional life. My journey reflects a blend of dedication, hard work, and a passion for continuous learning and self-improvement.
