This media page will mostly be based on the principles of Never giving up. Because my whole life have been basically been built on this foundation. Things pretty much don't always work out immediately or as you have planned or envisage it but if you pesevere and never give up you will definately get the result you have worked so hard for.

LEARN, UNLEARN, RELEARN

As we all know we can never stop learning, we practically learn new things everyday. They is a popular saying by Michael Scott "The day we stop learning is the day we die". A key part of personal development is being able to learn, unlearn and relearn. In the world of technology where new innovations and softwares are being released constantly, processes are being updated constantly, if you are not willing to learn, unlearn and relearn then you simply cannot cope. I constantly remind myself of this and try to live by this principles as well.

REPETITION

Over time I have understood the power of repetition when it comes to learning anything. I do by learning and as funny as it sounds, I love it when i usually hit a roadblock because it makes me to Keep repetiting the process in order to figure out the issue/bug and this allows me the opportunity to learn even more new ideas or concepts during this process.

Repetition is the foundation of mastery. Each time you revisit a concept, skill, or practice, you strengthen your understanding, refine your technique, and build confidence. It's through repetition that knowledge becomes second nature, transforming complex ideas into effortless actions. The more you repeat, the more you grow until success becomes inevitable. Keep at it, because every repetition is a step closer to excellence.
